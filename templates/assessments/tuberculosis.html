Tuberculosis Risk Assessment

Evaluate your TB risk factors

No Tests Required

This assessment evaluates your TB risk based on symptoms, exposure history, and risk factors. You don't need any prior medical tests to complete this assessment.

Common TB symptoms:

  • Persistent cough (lasting 3+ weeks)
  • Coughing up blood or sputum
  • Chest pain
  • Unintentional weight loss
  • Fatigue or weakness
  • Fever
  • Night sweats
  • Loss of appetite

Have you been in close contact with someone who has active TB, or lived/worked in areas with high TB rates?

Do you have conditions that weaken your immune system (HIV, diabetes, cancer treatment, organ transplant, certain medications)?
