PHQ-9 Depression Screening

No Tests Required

This is the PHQ-9 (Patient Health Questionnaire-9), a widely used screening tool for depression. It's based on your own observations and feelings - no medical tests or lab work needed. Simply answer based on how you've been feeling over the past 2 weeks.
