Hepatitis B Risk Assessment

Evaluate your Hepatitis B risk factors

No Tests Required

This assessment evaluates your Hepatitis B risk based on vaccination status, exposure, and risk factors. You don't need any prior medical tests to complete this assessment.

Hepatitis B vaccination is typically given in 3 doses and provides long-term protection.

Have you been exposed to Hepatitis B through contact with infected blood, unprotected sex, sharing needles, or from an infected mother at birth?

Common Hepatitis B symptoms:

  • Fatigue
  • Yellowing of skin or eyes (jaundice)
  • Dark urine
  • Abdominal pain
  • Loss of appetite
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Joint pain
  • Fever

Note: Many people with Hepatitis B have no symptoms, especially in chronic cases.

Risk factors include:

  • Unprotected sex with multiple partners
  • Sharing needles or injection equipment
  • Healthcare work with exposure to blood
  • Living with someone who has Hepatitis B
  • Travel to areas with high Hepatitis B rates
