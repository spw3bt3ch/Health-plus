Diabetes Risk Assessment

Evaluate your diabetes risk factors

No Tests Required

This assessment evaluates your diabetes risk based on age, family history, symptoms, BMI, and lifestyle factors. You don't need any prior blood glucose tests to complete this assessment.

Common diabetes symptoms:

  • Increased thirst
  • Frequent urination
  • Extreme hunger
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow-healing sores
  • Frequent infections
  • Tingling or numbness in hands/feet

If you don't know your BMI, you can take our BMI assessment first, or select "Normal weight" if you're unsure.
