Liver Health Assessment

Evaluate your liver health risk factors

No Tests Required

This assessment evaluates your liver health risk based on symptoms, lifestyle factors, and medical history. You don't need any prior liver function tests to complete this assessment.

Common liver problem symptoms:

  • Yellowing of skin or eyes (jaundice)
  • Abdominal pain or swelling
  • Dark urine
  • Pale or clay-colored stools
  • Chronic fatigue
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Loss of appetite
  • Easy bruising or bleeding
  • Itchy skin
  • Swelling in legs and ankles

Excessive alcohol consumption can damage the liver over time.

Are you taking medications that may affect the liver (some pain relievers, antibiotics, cholesterol medications, etc.)?
