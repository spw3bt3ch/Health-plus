COVID-19 Risk Assessment

Evaluate your COVID-19 risk factors

No Tests Required

This assessment evaluates your COVID-19 risk based on symptoms, exposure, and risk factors. You don't need any prior medical tests to complete this assessment.

Common COVID-19 symptoms:

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea

Have you been in close contact (within 6 feet for 15+ minutes) with someone who tested positive for COVID-19 in the past 14 days?

Do you have conditions that may increase COVID-19 risk (diabetes, heart disease, lung disease, weakened immune system, obesity, etc.)?

⚠️ Important: If you have severe symptoms like difficulty breathing, persistent chest pain, confusion, or bluish lips/face, seek immediate medical attention.
